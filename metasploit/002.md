## 🧪 Hands-on Practice 2
In this second hands-on practice, the goal is to scan the target machine (Blue), identify running services, and determine which vulnerability can be exploited. The focus here is not on completing a CTF, but on understanding the process step by step: from scanning and analysis to selecting and using the appropriate exploit.

To investigate potential known vulnerabilities, we run an advanced `nmap` scan using:
- `-sV` detects service versions  
- `-vv` increases verbosity to get more detailed output
